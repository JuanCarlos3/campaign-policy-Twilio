SMS Campaign Policy

Project Purpose

This SMS campaign is used for an internal research and development project involving AI-assisted communication workflows. The purpose of the project is to test how AI agents can coordinate structured text-message conversations between project-owned phone numbers and approved team participants.

Messages may be used to simulate scenarios such as task coordination, maintenance request handling, status updates, approval flows, and other research-related communication workflows.

This campaign is not used for public marketing, advertising, lead generation, cold outreach, or mass messaging.

Authorized Participants

SMS messages are only sent to phone numbers that are either:

1. Owned or controlled by the project team;
2. Assigned to internal AI agents used for testing;
3. Owned by members of the project team; or
4. Explicitly approved for participation in the research project.

All participants are part of the project and have consented to receive SMS messages related to testing and development.

Consent and Opt-In

Participants are added to the SMS workflow only after giving permission to participate in the project. Consent may be collected through direct written confirmation, project onboarding, internal team communication, or another documented approval process.

By participating, users understand that they may receive SMS messages related to internal testing, research workflows, AI-agent simulations, and project coordination.

We do not purchase contact lists, scrape phone numbers, or send messages to people who have not agreed to participate.

Message Content

Messages may include:

* Internal project testing messages
* AI-agent workflow simulations
* Research and development updates
* Test tenant, owner, vendor, or team communication scenarios
* Status updates related to simulated project tasks
* Follow-up questions or approvals within the testing workflow

Messages will not include prohibited content, deceptive content, phishing attempts, financial scams, illegal activity, or unrelated promotional advertising.

Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on active testing. Participants may receive multiple messages during a testing session, but messaging is limited to internal project use.
